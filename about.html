Hello, I am Claudia Londono, a User Experience Engineer and Entrepreneur...

I am passionate about creating engaging and easy to use web applications.  My passion for understanding customer needs along with my love for engineering and web design led me to create TotumOnline.

TotumOnline is a digital startup dedicated to solving customer problems with web applications and user experiences design. I have worked with customers and delivered web applications for fashion, fitness, non-profits, education, fintech, healthcare, law, and accounting firms.

In my free time, I enjoy giving back and volunteering with Accenture’s CoderDojo Foundation, an organization dedicated to introducing kids around the world to computer programming. Through CoderDojo, I give programming lessons to kids in my home country of Colombia.

When I’m not designing user experiences and web applications, I am reading books, enjoying life outdoors, and spending time with my family.
